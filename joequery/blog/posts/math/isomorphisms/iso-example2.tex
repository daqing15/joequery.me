We can verify that $\phi$ is 1-1 and onto, but it turns out that $\phi$ is not operation
preserving. To demonstrate, let $x,y \in G$. Then
\begin{align*}
\phi(xy) &= (x+y)^{3} \\
&= (x^{2} + 2xy + y^{2})(x+y) \\
&\ne x^{3} + y^{3} = \phi(x)\phi(y)
\end{align*}
